Local Shelters

Young-Williams Animal Center

Young-Williams Animal Center is dedicated to the humane care and placement of homeless animals in Knoxville and surrounding areas. They provide a wide range of services, including adoptions, spay/neuter programs, and community outreach. With two adoption centers, they are a major resource for animal welfare in the region.

Address: 3201 Division St, Knoxville, TN 37919

Phone: (865) 215-6599

Hours: Monday-Saturday 10 AM - 6 PM, Sunday 12 PM - 6 PM

Humane Society of Tennessee Valley

The Humane Society of Tennessee Valley is committed to preventing animal cruelty and finding loving homes for adoptable pets. They offer adoption services, low-cost veterinary care, and educational programs to promote responsible pet ownership. They focus on the well being of animals in the greater Knoxville area.

Address: 6717 Kingston Pike, Knoxville, TN 37919

Phone: (865) 573-9675

Hours: Tuesday-Saturday 11 AM - 6 PM

Visit Website

Knox County Animal Control

Knox County Animal Control is responsible for enforcing animal control laws and providing shelter for stray and abandoned animals. They work to reunite lost pets with their owners and facilitate adoptions. They also handle animal welfare complaints within Knox County.

Address: 3201 Division St, Knoxville, TN 37919

Phone: (865) 215-6599

Hours: Monday - Friday 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM
